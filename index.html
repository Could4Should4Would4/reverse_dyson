Dyson Sphere

A Dyson Sphere is a hypothetical megastructure that encompasses a star and captures a large percentage of its solar power OUTPUT.

Reverse Dyson

So a Reverse Dyson Sphere would encompasses a planet and captures a large percentage of its solar power INPUT.

The major advantage here, in terms of feasibility, would be the size and distance. (The sun being a million times larger than the earth and 90 million miles away).

Reverse Dyson + Sun Dimming

Basically, create XXXXXXXXXXL solar panel awnings, and attach them to the already orbiting satellites (or create purpose-specific satellites).

This would super-size our solar capacity, and the awnings would also shade the planet.

All concepts here already exist, including “sun-dimming” (reflecting the sun in order to cool the earth.). This would be a “two birds, one stone” kind of thing.

A bonus step to make this a "four birds" kind of solution, would be to use the energy collected by the Reverse-Dyson to primarily power Carbon-Removing Machines, and Reverse Osmosis Water Desalination Plants. Since the biggest hurdles of these two efforts are their energy consumption.
